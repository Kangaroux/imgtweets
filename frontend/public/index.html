Background

I started making imgtweets after I got frustrated trying to find posts from user's timelines. Around the same time, Twitter was also really starting to lockout people who didn't have an account.

There are a lot of really cool creators on Twitter who share their work. However, it's often difficult to find what you're looking for. I wanted a way to browse Twitter without having to use Twitter.

It makes me happy to see how many people came to use the site. I didn't post about it or try spreading the word, so the fact that over 100k people were able to find it on their own is really cool. It's fulfilling to know that I wasn't the only person who was looking for something like this.
